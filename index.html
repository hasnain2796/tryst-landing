How it works

1) Match with intent

Profiles capture date vibe, time of day, and food options (halal/vegan/kosher etc.).

2) Tap “Ask Out”

One tap politely asks for a date. If they accept, both of you jump to a booking screen.

3) Book in minutes

Pick a slot and a venue category. Confirm. Done.

What makes Tryst different
